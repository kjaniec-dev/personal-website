A. Ink + Cobalt

Recommended. Modern product/agency language with a disciplined premium edge.

Pros

  • Feels current, technical, and commercially credible
  • Strong in light mode with clean dark-mode extension
  • Supports stronger hierarchy without looking loud

Cons

  • Needs good typography and spacing to avoid generic SaaS energy

B. Graphite + Brass

More premium and elegant, but a little less obviously modern-product.

Pros

  • Distinctive and expensive-looking
  • More character than a standard blue-accent portfolio

Cons

  • Risks reading more editorial/luxury than engineering
  • Harder to keep sharp and modern across all pages

C. Night Studio

More expressive and striking, but also the highest-risk direction.

Pros

  • Memorable and visually forceful
  • Works well for hero drama and motion

Cons

  • Dark-first can hurt readability and professional neutrality
  • Too much visual personality for your current positioning
