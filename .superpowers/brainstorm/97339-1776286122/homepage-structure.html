Recommended Structure

This is the proposed homepage and navigation model for the redesign: senior-engineer positioning first, then proof, work, writing, and contact.

Homepage Wireframe
1. Hero
Strong headline around senior technical depth, short supporting statement, primary CTA to Projects/About, secondary CTA to Writing.
2. Proof Strip
Years of experience, core strengths, selected companies/domains/technologies.
3. Featured Work
2–3 stronger project cards with outcome framing, role, stack, and challenge.
4. Technical Writing
Recent posts presented as evidence of thinking, not just a generic blog feed.
5. About / Working Style
Short section on how you think, build, and collaborate.
6. Contact CTA
Clean final section for opportunities, collaboration, and contact.
Navigation + Content Priorities
Primary Nav
Home | Projects | Writing | About | Contact
Secondary / Lower Priority
FAQ, Tags, Learning stay in the site, but move out of the top-level spotlight.
Homepage Job
Explain who you are quickly, prove technical depth, surface your best work, then offer clear next paths.
Key Shift From Current Site
Less “starter-blog with portfolio sections,” more intentional productized personal brand for a senior engineer.
